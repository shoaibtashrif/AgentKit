APPOINTMENT INFORMATION - NORTHVIEW PAIN MANAGEMENT CENTER

SCHEDULING APPOINTMENTS

How to Schedule:
- Phone: (555) 123-4567 (Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
- Online: Visit our patient portal at www.northviewpain.com/portal
- Fax: (555) 123-4568 (for physician referrals)

New Patient Appointments:
New patients typically require a 60-minute initial consultation. Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration paperwork. You can download forms from our website to save time.

What to Bring to Your First Appointment:
- Photo ID and insurance card
- List of current medications
- Relevant medical records and imaging (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans)
- Completed new patient forms (if not done online)
- List of questions or concerns

Follow-up Appointments:
Follow-up visits are typically 20-30 minutes. Please bring:
- Updated medication list
- Any new medical records
- Pain diary (if keeping one)

Cancellation Policy:
We require 24-hour notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a $50 fee. We understand emergencies happen - please call us as soon as possible.

Wait Times:
We strive to see patients on time. Average wait times are 10-15 minutes. For procedures, plan for 45-60 minutes including preparation and recovery.

Urgent Concerns:
If you have an urgent pain issue, please call our office. We reserve same-day and next-day appointment slots for urgent cases.

Office Hours:
Monday - Thursday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM (for procedures only)
Sunday: Closed

After-Hours Care:
For emergencies after hours, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For urgent but non-emergency questions, call our main line and press 9 to reach the on-call provider.
