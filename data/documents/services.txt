SERVICES OFFERED AT NORTHVIEW PAIN MANAGEMENT CENTER

1. MEDICATION MANAGEMENT
Our physicians work with patients to develop safe and effective medication regimens, including:
- NSAIDs and acetaminophen
- Muscle relaxants
- Neuropathic pain medications
- Opioid therapy (when appropriate, with careful monitoring)
- Topical pain medications

2. INTERVENTIONAL PROCEDURES
We offer minimally invasive procedures to target pain at its source:
- Epidural steroid injections
- Facet joint injections
- Nerve blocks
- Radiofrequency ablation
- Trigger point injections
- Joint injections (knee, shoulder, hip)
- Spinal cord stimulation

3. PHYSICAL THERAPY
Our in-house physical therapy department provides:
- Therapeutic exercises
- Manual therapy
- Ultrasound and electrical stimulation
- Aquatic therapy
- Ergonomic training
- Home exercise programs

4. REGENERATIVE MEDICINE
Advanced treatment options including:
- Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy
- Stem cell therapy
- Prolotherapy

5. DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES
- X-ray imaging
- Fluoroscopy-guided procedures
- Diagnostic nerve blocks
- EMG/NCS studies

6. COMPLEMENTARY THERAPIES
- Acupuncture
- Massage therapy
- Mindfulness and relaxation training
- Nutritional counseling

All treatments are tailored to each patient's specific condition, medical history, and treatment goals.
