BILLING INFORMATION

Co-pays and Deductibles:
- Co-pays are due at the time of service
- We accept cash, check, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, AmEx)
- Payment plans are available for high deductibles

Self-Pay Options:
For patients without insurance or with high out-of-pocket costs, we offer:
- Discounted self-pay rates (typically 20-30% off standard charges)
- Payment plans with 0% interest for balances over $500
- CareCredit healthcare financing

Typical Costs (without insurance):
- Initial consultation: $250-350
- Follow-up visit: $150-200
- Epidural injection: $800-1200
- Physical therapy session: $100-150
- Nerve block: $600-900

Insurance Pre-authorization:
Many procedures require pre-authorization from your insurance. Our staff will:
- Submit authorization requests on your behalf
- Follow up with insurance companies
- Notify you once authorization is obtained
- Work with you to appeal denials if necessary
